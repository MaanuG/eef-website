Our Story

Empowering Education Foundation, Non-Profit Organization

The Empowering Education Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging educational gaps through student-led tutoring initiatives. Founded through the passion and collaboration of high school students, our organization strives to create a future where every learner has the opportunity to succeed regardless of circumstance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a lasting impact on students around the world by inspiring a lifelong pursuit of knowledge. Through accessible education, mentorship, and cultural exchange, we aim to empower the next generation of learners and leaders.

  • Raising Education Levels
  • Promoting Cultural Exchange

Our Initiative

Evergreen High School

Telangana, India

Empowering Education Foundation's team of approximately 20 dedicated tutors develops English and Mathematics curricula for students in Grades 6–10 at Evergreen High School.

Through weekly online instruction, mentorship, and engaging lessons, our volunteers strengthen academic foundations while inspiring curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

Who We Are

Troy High School Volunteers

Fullerton, California

Empowering Education Foundation is led entirely by students from Troy High School in Fullerton, California.

Our volunteers range from 9th through 12th grade and participate in some of the nation's most rigorous academic pathways.

  • Advanced Placement (AP)
  • International Baccalaureate (IB)
  • Cambridge Program
  • Honors & STEM Programs

Together, we share one goal: using our education, experience, and passion to expand educational opportunities for students across the globe.
